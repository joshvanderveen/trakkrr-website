Process

Hardware to insight.

01

Install

A rugged Trakkrr device connects directly to your machine. It reads from the machine's own data interfaces — pulling location, telemetry, and operational signals from the source.

02

Stream

Each device sends real-time data to your secure Trakkrr dashboard. Location, usage, machine state, event data — continuously updated, always accessible.

03

Act

Monitor individual machines or compare across your operation. Get alerts, plan maintenance, review history, and make decisions backed by real data instead of guesswork.
