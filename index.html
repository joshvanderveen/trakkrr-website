How It Works

From hardware to insight

1

We install the hardware

A rugged Trakkrr device connects directly to your machine. It reads from the machine's own data interfaces — pulling location, telemetry, and operational signals.

2

Data streams to the cloud

Each device sends real-time data to your secure Trakkrr dashboard. Location, usage, machine state, and event data — continuously updated.

3

You see what matters

Track individual machines or compare across your entire fleet. Get alerts, plan maintenance, review history, and make decisions with real data.
