How It Works

From install to insight in three steps

1

Install the hardware

Our rugged device connects directly to your machine's systems. Support for CAN, Modbus, and standard interfaces means deeper data from day one.

2

Connect to the cloud

Each device streams location, usage, and telemetry data to your secure Trakkrr dashboard. Real-time visibility across your entire fleet.

3

Monitor and control

See all your machines in one view. Track usage, get alerts, plan maintenance, and make better decisions with real operational data.
